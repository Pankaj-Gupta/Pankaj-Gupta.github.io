Some of my Projects and Interns :

  1. Coordinator, Android App Development (Internship done under Svagatagami Association, NPO)
    Android Apps were developed for children with problems like mental retardation, autism, hyperactivity & Attention Deficit Disorder. Apps won 1st prize in SoCha competition of E-Summit, IIT Kanpur
  2. Research Crawler (Project done as a Winter Internship under a Startup)
    Research papers were collected through crawling of Bing and Spider-crawl and then they were clustered to discover patterns

  4. Modelling of Earth's Gravitational Field (Project done under Professor Akash Anand, Department of Mathematics & Statistics, IIT Kanpur)

  6. Connexion (App developed in Yahoo HackU-2013 ,IIT Kanpur)
    Implemented a Web-scraping based model in python in which given any two movie-personalities, it shows the connections between them in form of common movies, significant years or any other relation.
  7. Carrom (Project done under Programming Club ,IIT Kanpur)
    AI based implementation of Carrom game in Python using pygame module
  8. Ping Pong Game (Project done under Professor Piyush P. Kurur, Department of Computer Science & Engineering, IIT Kanpur) Implementation of Ping Pong Game using pygame module of Python.
  9. 3-D Building Modelling (Project done under MAAC Animation and Animation Club, IIT Kanpur)
    Complete 3d model of Academic Affairs building of IIT Kanpur was made using 3ds-max software.
  10. Calculator (Made in Takneek'11 Electronics Club, IIT Kanpur)

  11. Wall following Robot (Made in Techkriti under Robotics Club, IIT Kanpur)

  12. Chariot-model (Made under course TA201, IIT Kanpur)

  13. Mixer-grinder (Made under course TA202, IIT Kanpur)
